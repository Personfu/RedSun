# RedSun FLLC Red-Team Education Lane

RedSun should be treated as a themed education lane: aggressive visuals, careful scope, and clean reporting.

## Content direction

- Threat-hunter style visuals.
- Short lessons on attacker mindset and defender evidence.
- Synthetic examples only.
- Strong remediation notes.
- Clear connection back to FLLC services and CyberWorld labs.

## Module ideas

1. Recon map reading with fictional infrastructure.
2. Detection engineering from simulated logs.
3. Patch validation scorecards.
4. Incident timeline reconstruction.
5. Executive report translation.

## Boundary

No live exploitation, no credential theft, no persistence, no evasion, no destructive code. Keep it sharp, visual, and defensible.
